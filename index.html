Elevator Test

There is an elevator in a building with a certain amount of floors. This elevator can take a max number of people at a time or max of total weight. Given that a set of people and their weight and the floor they need to stop, how many stops has the elevator taken to serve all the people? Consider the elevator serves in “first come first serve” basis and the order for the queue can not be changed.

Create a function based off the following criteria in javascript. Make sure you consider all values and possible limits provided in each example.

Example 1:

This array has the list of people waiting in line for the elevator with their weights and floor they need to go too. The array is listed in the same order as the line. The elevator has a max capicty of 400 pounds and can take a max of 2 people at a time. The building has 6 total floors.

const line = [{ weight:150, floor: 2, },{ weight:200, floor: 3, },{ weight:120, floor: 5, },{ weight:80, floor: 2, },{ weight:180, floor: 4, },{ weight:170, floor: 4, }];
